Verdict layers bundled: CAA restricted airspace (限航區, 2018 KML) + approximate airport proximity circles. NOT included: county/city announced zones, national parks, temporary NOTAMs (county zones are published only as PDFs — no machine-readable source yet). DJI GEO zones are separate from the law: a DJI drone may refuse takeoff even where flying is legal — use the DJI link to check. Reference only — always confirm on the official CAA map (drone.caa.gov.tw) before flying. Default legal ceiling: 400 ft AGL.
