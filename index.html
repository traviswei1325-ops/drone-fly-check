🛸 Drone Fly Check

Paste a location, get coordinates + a live can-I-fly verdict from official CAA data.

Tip: in the Google Maps app, long-press a spot → share → paste the link here. If it's a short link (maps.app.goo.gl), open it in a browser first and copy the full URL. You can also just tap the map below.
Open in Google Maps Check on CAA drone map ↗ Check DJI GEO zone ↗

Every check queries the official CAA drone-map server live (limits, county zones, temporary airspace). If offline, bundled layers give an estimate: CAA restricted airspace, official airport/heliport polygons, and national park boundaries. DJI GEO zones are separate from the law: a DJI drone may refuse takeoff even where flying is legal — use the DJI link to check. Reference only — always confirm on the official CAA map (drone.caa.gov.tw) before flying. Default legal ceiling: 400 ft AGL.
